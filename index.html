Trips inspired by you

We know that travel is about more than just flying somewhere or staying in a nice hotel, it's about  experiencing the world and having adventures. Waytopia is for travelers who love to have new experiences, parents who want to share the world with their kids, and friends who love to see the world together.

How it works

1. Create your travel profile

Add your travel preferences: what you like to do and how you like to do it, who you tend to travel with, and your budget.

2. Get Recommendations

Waytopia scans the web, compiles analytics to give you a better list of end-to-end recommendations for places to go, how to get there, where to stay, and what to do.

3. Book your adventure

You can book your entire trip with Waytopia. You save time, money, and you get to spend more of it on your adventure.
